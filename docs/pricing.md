# Pricing

## Subscription Plans

### Free Plan

Get started at no cost:

- Signup bonus credits
- Daily credits (24h expiry)
- Public preview
- Basic features

### Pro Plan

For creators who need more power:

- Higher signup bonus
- Monthly credit grants (1 month carryover)
- AI image generation
- Custom domains (100k requests free, $30 per additional 1M)
- Private preview *(coming soon)*
- Priority support

*Visit [hando.dev/pricing](https://hando.dev/pricing) for current plans and pricing.*

## Upgrading Your Plan

### How to Upgrade

1. Click your profile icon
2. Select **Account** â†’ **Subscription** tab
3. Choose your desired plan
4. Complete payment

### What Happens When You Upgrade

- **Immediate:** Receive signup bonus credits for the new tier
- **Monthly (Pro):** Receive monthly credits on the 1st

### Managing Your Subscription

Access the customer portal to:
- View billing history
- Update payment method
- Download invoices
- Cancel subscription

## Downgrading & Cancellation

### What Happens When You Downgrade

When downgrading from Pro to Free:

1. **Custom domains are removed** - All connected custom domains will be disconnected
2. **Features restricted** - Pro features become unavailable

### Cancellation Policy

- Cancel anytime from the customer portal
